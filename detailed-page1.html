Flame weasel's War dance

This was an illustrator assignment for the Graphic Design course I went to, where we had to draw an animal
using a specific method with the shape tools.

I decided to go with a weasel and was thinking about how I could make it look more interesting and fun, turns out weasel do this thing called "The war dance".
They will jump around a lot in excitement and are usually very clusmy apperently while doing the dance, hitting and colliding things as they jump.

Sooooo.... Why not make the weasel out of fire jumping around the forest and causing chaos? I thought it'd look pretty cool atleast and it was fun to work on so I'm happy with it!
